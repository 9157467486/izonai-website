The one-time setup fee covers installation, configuration, data migration, staff training and customization of the software to your specific business needs. After setup, you only pay the monthly subscription.

Can I get a free demo before buying? ▼

Yes! We offer a free demo for all our products. Contact us on WhatsApp and we will set up a live demo tailored to your business within 24 hours. No commitment required.

How long does setup and delivery take? ▼

Basic websites: 3-5 days. E-commerce: 7-10 days. GymPro/Salon software: 2-5 days. Custom AI projects: 7-14 days. We handle everything and provide full training to your team.

Does GymPro support biometric integration? ▼

Yes! GymPro supports biometric integration including attendance with Biometric devices, RFID, and Face Recognition. Available as an add-on for Pro and Premium plans.

Yes, you can upgrade at any time. We migrate all existing data seamlessly with no downtime. A small upgrade setup fee may apply depending on the changes required.

Is my business data 100% safe? ▼

Absolutely. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit, stored on secure cloud servers with daily auto backups. We never share your data with any third party ever.
