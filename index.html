FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the one-time setup fee for? ▼

The one-time setup fee covers installation, configuration, data migration, staff training and customization of the software to your specific business needs. After setup, you only pay the monthly subscription.

Can I try the software before buying? ▼

Yes! We offer a free demo for all our software products. Contact us on WhatsApp and we'll set up a live demo tailored to your business within 24 hours.

Is the billing software GST compliant? ▼

Absolutely. Our billing software is fully GST compliant and generates GSTR-1 and GSTR-3B reports automatically. It supports all GST slabs and HSN codes.

How long does setup take? ▼

Most software setups are completed within 2–5 business days. Custom AI projects typically take 7–14 days. We handle all the setup and provide training for your team.

Can I switch or upgrade plans later? ▼

Yes, you can upgrade at any time. We migrate all existing data seamlessly with no downtime. A small upgrade setup fee may apply depending on the changes required.

Is my business data safe? ▼

Data security is our top priority. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit, stored on secure cloud servers with daily backups. We never share your data with third parties.
